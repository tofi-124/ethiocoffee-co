Ethiopian Coffee

Single-origin excellence from the birthplace of coffee

Ethiopia's diverse growing regions produce coffees with distinctive flavor profiles. From the floral notes of Yirgacheffe to the bold fruitiness of Guji, each origin tells its own story.
