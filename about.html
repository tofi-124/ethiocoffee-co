Our Story

About Ethio Coffee Co.

Rooted in tradition, crafted in Toronto

Our Heritage

Ethiopia gave the world coffee. According to legend, a goat herder named Kaldi discovered coffee after noticing his goats became energetic after eating berries from a certain tree. This discovery, made in the ancient coffee forests of Ethiopia, changed the world forever.

At Ethio Coffee Co., we carry this heritage with pride. Based in Toronto, we specialize exclusively in Ethiopian coffee because we believe in celebrating the origin—where coffee truly began.

Our Mission

Connecting Toronto to Ethiopia, One Cup at a Time

We're more than just a roaster—we're a bridge between Ethiopian coffee farmers and Toronto's vibrant cafe scene. Our mission is to bring the authentic, complex flavors of Ethiopian coffee to local coffee shops and micro cafes who share our passion for quality.
